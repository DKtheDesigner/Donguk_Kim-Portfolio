Branding Youth Ahead

This Cross Campus Project successfully showcased its achievement at the prestigious "Robert and Frances Fullerton Museum of Art" as an Exhibition. As the art director, I played a crucial role in assisting a Norco College Music student in developing the music label and their album. The project aimed to establish transfer pathways for community college students to CSUSB and foster collaboration among campuses. The project's outcomes exemplified the power of cross-campus collaboration, student growth, and the transformative nature of arts education.
