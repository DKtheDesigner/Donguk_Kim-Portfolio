Graphic / Marketing Recreation and Wellness Center

As a Graphic Design Student Employee at CSUSB's Recreation and Wellness Center, I created and executed design ideas for a wide range of promotional materials. I actively contributed to the development of marketing campaigns, maintained the marketing calendar, and established effective communication with program coordinators to ensure deadlines were met and departmental needs were addressed. Collaborating with the webmaster, I also worked on website graphics. In addition to these responsibilities, I handled various other assigned duties, all while promoting the center's key features of adventure, aquatics, fitness, intramurals, sports, health, and well-being.
