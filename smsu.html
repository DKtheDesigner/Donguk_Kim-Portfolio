Graphic / Marketing Santos Manuel Student Union

During my time as a Graphic Design Student Employee at the Santos Manuel Student Union (SMSU) of, I actively contributed to the campus's vibrant atmosphere by creating visually captivating designs. Working closely with event coordinators and marketing teams, I crafted eye-catching promotional materials such as posters, flyers, and digital graphics to effectively communicate event details and themes. My designs aimed to enhance the campus experience, promote diversity, and foster personal growth for students, faculty, staff, and visitors.
