Branding Creative Coding Club

As the founder and president, I assumed full responsibility for the branding and graphics of Creative Coding Club. Our focus of the club was on creating an enriching environment for students to develop their creative coding skills, collaborate on projects, and connect with like-minded individuals. We emphasized inclusivity, diverse thinking, and providing valuable resources to students from diverse backgrounds. Additionally, we fostered connections with the club's alumni network and industry professionals. Through meticulous attention to visual identity, messaging, and promotional materials, the branding project successfully positioned Creative Coding Club as an inviting and empowering community for passionate students.
