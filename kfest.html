Branding Korean Festival

As the Lead Graphic Designer, I spearheaded the branding of the CSUSB Annual Korean Festival. This large-scale event received substantial sponsorship, including support from KCCLS. The project aimed to promote peace and unity by incorporating traditional Korean elements, such as patterns and motifs. The designs showcased cultural identity, pride, and acceptance of diversity, creating an inclusive and vibrant celebration.
