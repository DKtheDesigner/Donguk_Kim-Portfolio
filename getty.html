Motion Graphic Getty

The project involves the creation of a simple looping motion graphic promotional material using the beloved book "Mira Calligraphiae Monumenta," a facsimile edition of an exquisite Renaissance manuscript, published by Getty Publication. The main objective is to highlight the motion graphic's ability to engage the audience while preserving the integrity and beauty of the original artwork. The motion graphic has been approved by Getty Publication and selected for display on their social media channels.
